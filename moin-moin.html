MOIN-MOIN

DESCRIPTION

Moin Moin is a traditional Nigerian meal made from beans, onions, peppers, spices, and other ingredients. It is delicious, easy to make, and inexpensive.

INGREDIENTS

  • Black-eyed Peas or Honey Beans
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Crayfish
  • Vegetable Oil/Palm Oil
  • Fish
  • Salt

STEPS

  1. Soak The Beans
  2. Peel Your Beans
  3. Sieve the skin of the beans out
  4. Blend the beans and other Ingredients
  5. Turn the batter to a bowl
  6. Add fish chunks or liver
  7. Put the pot on fire and add boiled Water
  8. Arrange the moin moin in the pot and allow to steam for 45 minutes
  9. Carefully remove the moinmoin from the plate and enjoy
